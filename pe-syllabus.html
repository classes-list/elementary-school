🎯 Course Objectives

Aloha! I’m Gregg Kageyama, aka Mr. K. I teach PE to Kindergarteners and 1st graders. The overall objective of this course is to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and attitude to lead a physically active and healthy lifestyle throughout their lives.

We incorporate SHAPE America’s National Physical Education Standards as a foundation for our PE program.

🎽 Required Materials

  • Uniform: Students should come to school dressed in their PE uniform.
  • Athletic Shoes: Required for running, jumping, moving, and exercising. If lace-up shoes are worn, the student must know how to tie shoes.

📊 Grading Policy

Grade Scale

4
Extends

Always gives best effort. Exemplary respect for all. Fully understands and follows rules.

3
Proficient

Consistently participates. Shows respect to peers. Mostly understands and follows rules.

2
Developing

Sometimes participates. Occasionally shows respect. Limited understanding of rules.

1
Emerging

Rarely participates. Lacks respect towards others. Minimal understanding of rules.

🤝 Class Policies and Expectations

  • P.E. classes will occur twice a week.
  • All students are expected to give their best effort during all activities.
  • Display respect and sportsmanship towards classmates.
  • Learn the rules and regulations for all activities.
  • Be properly dressed in P.E. uniform and shoes.

🏃 Course Activities

A typical class will include:

1Warm-up or Instant Activity
2Lesson of the Day
3Game Play

The students will be in motion as much as possible. Music will be used throughout the year. At the end of each class, every student will conduct a quick self-assessment of his/her behavior and effort level.

Activities Will Include:

🏃 Locomotor Skills 🥎 Throwing & Catching 💃 Fitness & Dance 🥤 Cup Speed Stacking 🎳 Bowling ⛳ Golf 🏀 Basketball 🏈 Flag Football 🪢 Jump Rope ⚽ Soccer 🏐 Volleyball 🎯 Impossible Shot ✨ and more!
