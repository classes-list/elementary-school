Program Notes

  • Meets once a week during Q3 with Ms. Tacy (School Counselor).
  • Focus: self-awareness, empathy, growth mindset, friendship, anti-bullying, problem-solving, conflict resolution, and transition to intermediate school.
  • Lessons integrate the Mood Meter as a shared language for emotions.

Curriculum Map by Lesson

Nine weekly SEL / Wellness lessons taught by Ms. Tacy during Q3.

Lesson Pacing Description
Lesson 1: The Mood MeterQ3 · Week 1 of 9Introduce the Mood Meter, its scale, and how to name feelings.
Lesson 2: Feelings DetectivesQ3 · Week 2 of 9What is empathy? Reading others' emotions; we can feel different emotions about the same thing.
Lesson 3: Thought ConnectionsQ3 · Week 3 of 9How thoughts relate to feelings and actions; caterpillar thoughts (positive, growth-oriented) vs. worm thoughts (negative); growth mindset.
Lesson 4: What Makes a Good Friend?Q3 · Week 4 of 9Finding commonalities and identifying strengths in self and others.
Lesson 5: StereotypesQ3 · Week 5 of 9What are they and how can they be harmful.
Lesson 6: Battle the BulliesQ3 · Week 6 of 9What is bullying; reporting; assertiveness.
Lesson 7: Problem-Solving StepsQ3 · Week 7 of 9Stop, think, cool off, weigh consequences, take action.
Lesson 8: Talk It Out & Clear It UpQ3 · Week 8 of 9How to resolve conflicts, apologize, and make new choices next time.
Lesson 9: Transition to IntermediateQ3 · Week 9 of 9Growing, changing, and learning in new ways.
