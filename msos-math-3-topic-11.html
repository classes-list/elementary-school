📝 Topic 11: Operations Word Problems

Mrs. Won · 3rd Grade A · Mary, Star of the Sea School

Lesson 1: Two-Step Word Problems

Two-step problems ask you to do TWO operations. Sara bought 3 bags of 4 apples, then ate 2. Step 1: 3 × 4 = 12. Step 2: 12 − 2 = 10 apples left. Watch the ORDER!

📝 Practice Worksheet

1. 3 packs of 5 pens; 2 given away. Left = ?
2. 4 rows of 6 chairs; add 5 more. Total = ?
3. 5 × 8 stickers; share among 4. Each = ?
4. 2 boxes of 12 cookies; eat 4. Left = ?
5. Buy 6 books for $3 each; pay with $20. Change = ?
6. 3 × 7 marbles; give 5 away. Left = ?

Lesson 2: Reasonableness Checks

After solving, ASK: does the answer make sense? If Sara starts with 20 apples and eats some, she shouldn't END with 30. Rough-estimate first: 'about 20.' If your exact answer is way off, redo it.
